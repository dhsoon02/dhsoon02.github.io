Donghyeon Soon

I am a B.S. student in Computer & Electronics Engineering at DGIST, based in Daegu, South Korea. My interests lie in computer vision, artificial intelligence, 3D vision, and robotics. I am particularly interested in 3D/4D reconstruction, generative modeling, Gaussian Splatting, diffusion models, and animatable human/animal reconstruction.

My recent work has focused on animal reconstruction, dance generation, and generative 3D vision problems. I also enjoy building practical systems that connect geometric modeling with generative AI.
