Donghyeon Soon

I am a B.S. student in Computer & Electronics Engineering at DGIST, South Korea. My research interests lie in computer vision, generative AI, 3D vision, and robotics.

I am interested in building models that reconstruct, animate, and generate dynamic 3D/4D content, especially for humans and animals. My recent work focuses on 3D reconstruction, Gaussian Splatting, diffusion-based generation, and parametric human/animal modeling.

I am always open to research collaborations and internship opportunities.
